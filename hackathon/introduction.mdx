Here's a quick comparison of multimodal support across models:

| Model | Image | Audio | Video | Text |
|-------|--------|--------|--------|-------|
| Gemini | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| OpenAI | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Anthropic | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Groq | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Mistral | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |

For more details, see our [compatibility matrix](https://docs.agno.com/models/compatibility#multimodal-support).
