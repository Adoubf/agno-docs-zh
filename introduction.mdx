## What is phidata?

Phidata is **a toolkit for building human-like AI products.**

Phidata makes it possible to build AI specialists and have conversations with them.

## Use it to build

- **AI for your users:** Train an AI on customer data and improve user retention by providing a dedicated sales specialist for each user.
- **AI for your employees:** Train an AI on internal data to improve employee productivity.
- **Python Engineers:** Improve engineering productivity with an AI that can perform tasks by running python code.
- **Data Analysts:** Upskill your data team with an AI that can analyze data by writing and running SQL queries.
- **Marketing Analysts:** Improve your marketing with an AI that can provide marketing insights, copywriting and content ideas.
- **Stock Analysts:** and AI that can analyze stocks and research companies.

## How it works

Phidata gives you **pre-built templates** for AI products that you can run in minutes

- Create your codebase using a template: `phi ws create`
- Run your app locally: `phi ws up dev:docker`
- Run your app on AWS: `phi ws up prd:aws`
