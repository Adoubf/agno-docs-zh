## What is phidata?

**Phidata is a toolkit for building AI products using language models.**

It provides a human-like **Conversation** interface to build RAG, Autonomous and Multimodal applications. Conversations come with built-in memory, knowledge, storage, tools and make it possible to build AI applications with ease.

## Use phidata to build

- **AI for your product:** Train an AI on customer data and improve user experience.
- **AI for your employees:** Train an AI on internal data to improve employee productivity.
- **AI for data analysts:** Upskill your data team with an AI that can analyze data by writing and running SQL queries.
- **AI for marketing teams:** Improve your marketing with an AI that can provide marketing insights, copywriting and content ideas.

## How it works

Phidata gives you **pre-built** AI Apps that you can run in minutes

- Create your codebase using a template: `phi ws create`
- Run your app locally: `phi ws up dev:docker`
- Run your app on AWS: `phi ws up prd:aws`
