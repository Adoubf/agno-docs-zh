Large Language Models are machine-learning models that have been trained to understand natural language and code. They are quickly emerging as the building block of a new computing paradigm.

Phidata supports the following LLM providers:

- [OpenAI](/blocks/llm/openai)
- [OpenAI Like](/blocks/llm/openai-like)
- [Ollama](/blocks/llm/ollama)
- [AWS Bedrock](/blocks/llm/aws)
- [Together](/blocks/llm/together)
- [Anyscale](/blocks/llm/anyscale)
- [Anthropic Claude](/blocks/llm/claude)
- [Gemini](/blocks/llm/gemini)
- [Groq](/blocks/llm/groq)
- [Fireworks](/blocks/llm/fireworks)
- [Azure](/blocks/llm/azure)
- [Mistral](/blocks/llm/mistral)
- [Cohere](/blocks/llm/cohere)
